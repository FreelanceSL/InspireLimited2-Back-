MT4 Account Guide

1. Opening an Inspire Trade Account:

  • Visit our website and click on "Open New Account" option
  • Fill in the registration form with your personal information including name, email, and phone number

2. Download MT4 Platform:

  • After registration, you'll receive a download link for the MT4 platform for your computer or phone
  • Install the program easily following the simple setup process

3. Account Activation:

  • We may require verification documents such as ID or utility bill to confirm your identity
  • We ensure quick account activation once your documents are verified

4. Deposit Funds:

  • Choose your preferred deposit method from our range of secure payment options
  • Follow the simple steps to fund your trading account

5. Start Trading:

  • Log in to the MT4 platform using your provided account credentials
  • Begin your trading journey with our exceptional support team at your service
